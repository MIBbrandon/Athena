PROJECT ATHENA

An important part of quantum computers in the NISQ era is qubit routing. This means mapping the logical qubits of a given algorithm to the physical ones in the architecture of our quantum device in such a way that it optimally reduces the number of swaps required to implement the desired algorithm. Project Athena is an attempt to find the best architecture-agnostic algorithm and to visually walk through all of the swaps done.
